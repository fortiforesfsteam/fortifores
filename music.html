FortiMusic

It was under this name "FortiMusic" that I released 17 bits (tracks) from “Bits” to “Pixel Bit”.

One day, I just looked at my name and I didn't like the last word "Music", so I decided to change it to something else. I thought about it for a long time, and for some reason the Forest came to mind.

“Forti Forest”, I thought.
– I asked myself why "Forest".
– I don't know, so I just removed the “t” and got "Fores".
Now I am Forti Fores.
